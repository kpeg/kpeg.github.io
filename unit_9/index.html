About Kelly

Kelly is Curious. Creative. Visually-oriented. Experiences flow when lost in Photoshop. Edits scenery in her mind when out & about as if it were a photo.

Kelly prefers neatness & order. Unlike most creative types, Kelly is planned, orderly and does things in a consistent, conscientious and reliable manner.

This dichotomy means Kelly bridges the gap between creative and logical people. She provides order in chaos.

Kelly enjoys visiting new places, especially the outdoors. Arts & Crafting with friends. Rock climbing. Fine wine. Thai cuisine. When asked about favorites, the answer will always be trying something new.
